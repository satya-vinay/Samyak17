  • About KLU

    Where every seed is watered and reaped, the tree well-nourished and protected, one can find true greenery in the K L University an impeccable example where the students are well-tutored and invigorated into proficient engineers and managers. Standing tall with high-raised buildings amidst the green settings of a picturesque landscape, is the hub of true knowledge blended with culture. This has been a convergence of different arts, skills and talents of students and acts as a melting pot of such flairs and bring best out of them. The campus has been aptly named "Green Fields" and the splendid avenue of trees and gardens and it is considered as a divine temple where knowledge is worshipped and every student is treated with same care and nurtured to accomplish their goals. This centre of education saw many students come out in life with flying colours and some of them in unique fields of art and sport. The support and encouragement provided, nourished small ideas and created young entrepreneurs providing the environment for budding aspirants.

  • About Samyak

    SAMYAK is the biggest techno-management fete in all of Andhra Pradesh, which sees a confluence of students from all over the nation. This provides an avid opportunity for students to show their technical and cultural expertise and a platform to learn through practice. Filled with colours, painting the skies with fun and frolic, this two-day fest drives home lessons of social responsibility apart from technical knowledge. One can witness the sun rise beautified with technical workshops setting away with awe and exuberance silencing after cultural extravaganza. Now eight years old, this fest is all set to enthral and captivate with all its prominence.

  • Theme- Industry 4.0

    The world is open to learning, knowledge and opportunities. If this country was one to be looked upon as a personification of digitalization that would be the day when we can proudly proclaim that India has established the digital revolution: Industry 4.0. which signifies the 4 th industrial revolution. It is the stepping stone to change the structure of this country and a step towards global digitalization. More than just a flashy catchphrase, Industry 4.0 is an amalgam of trends and technologies promising to reshape the way things function. Machines being replaced by mere commands and money by a card, with the era of computers there lies a challenging future ahead. It first visualises the value shift by creation of a new story about how we want to live. We have energy technologies to power our civilization solar, wind, biomass, hydro e. t. c.… sun is shining in Germany, wind is blowing in Denmark so we can move that electricity through an integrated grid system so that energy can be supplied to the needy all times. The techno-management fete SAMYAK’17 provides a platform for one and all, to develop one’s dreams to digitize the country by adopting the theme INDUSTRY 4.0- DREAM, DIGITIZE, DEVELOP. It opens an opportunity for all to upgrade to meet the demands of the digitalizing country in the form of various workshops, competitions and events in confluence with various cultural activities projecting the cultural richness. Every workshop and competition is aimed at taking this country ahead in terms of development. In a line, it lays the foundation for the innovations of tomorrow.
